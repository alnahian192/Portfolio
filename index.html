01 — About

Discipline first, curiosity always

I'm Al Nahian — a recent Science-stream graduate of Dhaka Residential Model College, where I closed out my HSC with a GPA of 5.00 and my SSC with 4.94. For the last five years, design has been my daily practice: building visual identities and content systems for real organizations before I ever had a formal title for it.

Alongside design, I co-founded Ecopaws Foundation, an animal welfare organization based in Dhaka, and held leadership roles at DRMC MUNA — including Secretary of Hospitality and Assistant Secretary General of Security — where I learned to manage people, pressure, and details at once. I'm now applying that same rigor to computer science and machine learning, with my sights set on a fully-funded undergraduate seat abroad.
